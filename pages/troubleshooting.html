Troubleshooting

⚡ Power & Charging 🕹️ Controller Issues 📺 Display / TV Connection

Power & Charging Issues

  1. Check that the AC adapter is firmly plugged into the wall and the USB-C port.
  2. Try a different wall outlet.
  3. Check the USB-C port for debris and clean gently if needed.
  4. Force restart: hold Power for 12 seconds, then press Power to turn back on.

Tip: Only use Nintendo-approved AC adapters.

Controller Issues

  1. Re-attach the Joy-Con by sliding it down the rail until it clicks.
  2. To re-pair wirelessly: Settings → Controllers and Sensors → Disconnect Controllers.
  3. For stick drift: Settings → Controllers and Sensors → Calibrate Control Sticks.
  4. Update firmware: Settings → Controllers and Sensors → Update Controllers.

Note: Persistent drift may require hardware repair.

Display / TV Connection Problems

  1. Check that the HDMI cable is firmly connected to the dock and TV.
  2. Switch your TV to the correct HDMI input.
  3. Remove the console from the dock, wait 10 seconds, then re-insert.
  4. Try a different HDMI cable or HDMI port on the TV.

Tip: The dock must be powered via AC adapter to output to TV.
