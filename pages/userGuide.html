User Guide

Getting Started

Unboxing

  1. Remove the console, Joy-Con controllers, dock, HDMI cable, and AC adapter.
  2. Inspect all components for shipping damage.
  3. Slide both Joy-Cons onto the console until they click.

Charging the Console

  1. Connect the AC adapter to the USB-C port on the bottom of the console.
  2. A charging indicator will appear on screen. Full charge takes ~3 hours.

Using the Switch

Handheld Mode

  1. Attach both Joy-Cons to either side of the console.
  2. Press the Power button to turn on.
  3. Play using the built-in screen — no TV required.

Docked (TV) Mode

  1. Connect the HDMI cable from the dock to your TV.
  2. Plug the AC adapter into the dock.
  3. Slide the console into the dock. Switch your TV to the correct HDMI input.

Attaching Joy-Cons

  1. Align the Joy-Con rail with the groove on the side of the console.
  2. Slide downward until you hear a click.

Basic Navigation

Home Button

  1. Press the Home button (circle icon) on the right Joy-Con to return to the Home Menu.
  2. Hold the Home button to access quick settings (brightness, volume, sleep).

Game Cards vs Digital Games

  1. Game cards: Insert into the card slot on top of the console.
  2. Digital games: Purchase from the Nintendo eShop and download directly.
